You will hear examples of how practices automated reminders, follow-ups, and internal processes without losing their clinic’s unique voice.

Modernization does not require replacing everything at once. Instead, you will learn how to introduce improvements gradually, reduce risk, and build momentum. This session focuses on practical changes your team can implement right away to make your day run smoother.
